Privacy Policy — TryOn AI

Last updated: 2026-07-03

TryOn AI ("we", "the app") lets you preview appearance changes — clothing, hairstyles, beards, makeup, and tattoos — by combining a photo of yourself with a reference image. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Contact: papiw19@gmail.com

Summary

  • We process the photos you upload only to generate your try-on result.
  • We create an anonymous account automatically — no name, email, or phone required.
  • We use Google Firebase, Google Vertex AI (image generation), Google AdMob (rewarded ads), and Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

What we collect and why

1. Photos you provide

Your photo and the reference image(s) you choose, to produce the try-on. They are uploaded to secure Google Firebase Storage scoped to your account and sent to Google Vertex AI to generate the result. Uploads and results are stored under your account so your gallery/history work; you can request deletion.

2. Account identifier

An anonymous user ID created on first launch. If you use Sign in with Google, we also receive your Google account's basic identity so your credits/history carry over.

3. Credits and transactions

Your credit balance and a ledger of grants/uses. Managed on our server so it can't be tampered with.

4. Usage analytics and crash reports

Analytics events and crash/error reports via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics — including a device/app identifier and diagnostics, not your photos.

5. Advertising

Rewarded ads (Google AdMob) you can watch to earn credits. AdMob may use an advertising identifier per Google's policies. See Google advertising privacy.

Data security

Photos and results are stored in access-controlled storage scoped to your account (enforced by security rules); other users cannot read your files. Sensitive operations (credits, AI keys) run only on our server; the app never holds provider secrets. Traffic uses HTTPS/TLS.

Children

TryOn AI is not directed to children under 13 (or your region's age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account and data — in the app: open Settings → Delete Account to permanently erase your account, photos, results, history, and credits. You can also email papiw19@gmail.com. (Purchase receipts, which contain no photos and only an opaque store token, are retained as required for financial records.)
  • Analytics/ads: limit ad personalization via your device settings; you can reset your advertising id in Android settings.
  • Depending on your region (EEA/UK/California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data — contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
